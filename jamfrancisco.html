Jam Francisco

San Francisco's Music Lifestyle Brand

While working for The Orchard (Sony Music Entertainment) I was responsible for promoting artists, movies, and more from independent record labels. The first task that I was placed with was creating an organic lifestyle brand to attract the San Francisco music market. “Jam Francisco” became the name of the music blog that I used to generate interest in touring artists and promote new releases.

This brand spanned across 5 different social media platforms including Facebook, Instagram, Soundcloud, Twitter, and Tumblr. I was responsible for creating content and maintaining active engagement on these platforms throughout my time at The Orchard (20-40 times per campaign).

Marketing Campaigns

Each campaign started with a team game plan for the assignment. Each artist had its unique market and necessitated its own attention. For more mature audiences, I would target coffee shops, higher-end clothing retails, and promote the bands on older demographic social medias like Facebook and Twitter. For younger audiences, I would target hot spots around SFSU, trendy accounts like thrift shops, dive bars, and post on youth-heavy social medias like Instagram and Tumblr.

Your Next Favorite Band

As a team, we promoted releases and tour dates from The Orchard’s record label clients. We submitted reports directly to the labels. Clients included: Frenchkiss Records (Indie/Alternative), Daptone Records (Soul/Funk), LAW Records (Reggae/Rock), and many more.
